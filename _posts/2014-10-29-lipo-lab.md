In this prototype, we were asked to create a **mod** of an arcade game published before **1985**. Our team searched and agreed to base it on **Tapper**.

### Team Lipo Lab

![Team Lipo Lab]({{ '/assets/img/blog/2014/lipo-lab/team-lipo-lab.jpg' | relative_url }})

Our producers and artists came up with a game idea, **Lipo Lab**: doctors remove fat from patients at the hospital, and earn money from the collected fat (more fat, more money). We were asked to use **Flash** for development—and none of the engineers had used it before. All three engineers started learning Flash and each produced a first build with different APIs/engines. Integrating everything into one codebase was a tough job—but we did it. Our artists created artwork that matched the environment and looked great.

### 3D models

![3D models]({{ '/assets/img/blog/2014/lipo-lab/3d-models.png' | relative_url }})

![Character walk cycle]({{ '/assets/img/blog/2014/lipo-lab/character-walk-right.png' | relative_url }})

After two and a half weeks, the game was playable but still needed polish and integration. Our producer **Cameron** asked us to come in on a weekend to finish everything—and that weekend build came together well. Everyone arrived early and stayed until it was done.
