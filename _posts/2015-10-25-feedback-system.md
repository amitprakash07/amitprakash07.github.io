This is very simplistic system meant to provide feedback in the game. The idea was to give an option for the beta version users to provide their valuable feedback to us so that we can make some appropriate changes. The system is based on SMTP mail client system. I created an additional google SMTP client for our game which is being used in the game to submit and to send an emails to us after submitting the feedback.
