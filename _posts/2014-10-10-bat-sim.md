This was the first prototype of the semester. We were told we would be making different games throughout the semester in the class. Hope it will be fun!! Firstly, all of us were assigned to a particular team comprising a producer, artists, technical artists, and engineers. The team I was assigned to had five other members. In this prototype, we were told that we would be getting some toys and we needed to come up with a game idea out of it and build it using **MonoGame**.

### Team photo

![Team photo]({{ '/assets/img/blog/2014/bat-sim/team-photo.jpg' | relative_url }})

We got a rat, bat, and some spiders. All of us were confused what to make out of it. We discussed for about an hour and decided to make a **platformer** where our bat is the player: lost somewhere, it has to reach home through a cave with rats and spiders (it used all the creatures from the toys).

After a week, one of our engineers made the basic codebase and we were able to move characters in the game. We also decided to include additional features: **sonar** — the bat has a higher viewport when sonar is activated, otherwise a smaller viewport. With three and a half weeks of work, we were able to play the game on our build.
